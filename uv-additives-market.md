<p><strong>The UV Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Stabilizers</li><li>Light Stabilizer</li><li>UV Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV additives market comprises three main types: heat stabilizers, light stabilizers, and UV absorbers. Heat stabilizers protect polymers from thermal degradation during processing and usage. Light stabilizers enhance the durability of materials by preventing photodegradation caused by sunlight exposure. UV absorbers, on the other hand, absorb harmful ultraviolet radiation, converting it into less damaging energy and safeguarding the integrity of products. Together, these additives enhance the longevity and performance of materials used in various applications.</p></p>
