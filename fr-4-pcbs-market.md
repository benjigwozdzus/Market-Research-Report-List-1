<p><p>The FR-4 PCB market comprises three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multilayer PCBs. Single-sided FR-4 PCBs feature components on one side, making them cost-effective for simple applications. Double-sided FR-4 PCBs have circuits on both sides, allowing for more complex designs and higher component density. Multilayer FR-4 PCBs consist of multiple layers of circuitry, enabling sophisticated electronics with advanced functionality in compact spaces, ideal for high-performance applications in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive industries.</p></p>
