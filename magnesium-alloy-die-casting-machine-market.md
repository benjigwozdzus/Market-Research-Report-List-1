<p><ul><li>Cold Chamber Die Casting Machine</li><li>Hot Chamber Die Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium alloy die casting machine market primarily features two types: cold chamber and hot chamber die casting machines. Cold chamber machines are designed for alloy materials that require high melting temperatures, with molten metal poured into the mold from a separate chamber. In contrast, hot chamber machines, suitable for low melting point alloys, have a piston that injects molten metal directly from the melting pot into the mold, enhancing cycle times and efficiency. Each type serves specific production needs and applications.</p></p>
