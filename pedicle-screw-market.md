<p><strong>The Pedicle Screw Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyaxial</li><li>Monoaxial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pedicle screw market is primarily categorized into two types: polyaxial and monoaxial screws. Polyaxial screws allow multidirectional angling, providing greater flexibility in spinal alignment and reducing the risk of implant failure. They are often favored in complex spinal surgeries for their adaptability. Monoaxial screws, on the other hand, offer a fixed angle, ensuring stability and precise alignment during procedures. Both types serve essential roles in spinal fusion and stabilization, catering to diverse surgical needs and patient anatomies.</p></p>
