<p><strong>The Safety Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoder</li><li>Absolute Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety encoders market primarily includes two types: incremental encoders and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders measure changes in position based on movement from a defined starting point, making them ideal for applications requiring speed and direction detection. On the other hand, absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each shaft angle, ensuring precise location information even after power loss. Both encoder types are critical in enhancing the safety and reliability of automated systems in various industries.</p></p>
