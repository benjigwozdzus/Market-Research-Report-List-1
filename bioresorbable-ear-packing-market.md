<p><p>The bioresorbable ear packing market is divided into two main types: middle ear and outer ear. Middle ear packing is designed for applications following surgeries like tympanoplasty, providing support while gradually dissolving to minimize complications and promote healing. Outer ear packing focuses on protecting the external ear canal, often used after procedures like otoplasty. Both types utilize materials that safely absorb in the body over time, enhancing patient comfort and reducing the need for removal interventions.</p></p>
