<p><p>The cannabis extract market is diverse, featuring various extraction methods. Sub/Supercritical CO2 extraction utilizes pressure and temperature to efficiently separate compounds, preserving quality. Ethanol extraction employs food-grade ethanol, offering a cost-effective approach but may require additional purification. Hydrocarbon extraction uses solvents like butane or propane, ensuring high yield but raises safety concerns. Solvent-less extraction methods, such as heat and pressure, focus on purity without chemical solvents. Other methods encompass additional innovative techniques, catering to varied consumer preferences and product requirements.</p></p>
