<p><p>The NEV (New Energy Vehicle) charger market is primarily divided into two types: DC charging piles and AC charging piles. DC charging piles deliver high-speed charging by providing direct current, allowing electric vehicles to recharge quickly, typically in under an hour. In contrast, AC charging piles use alternating current and are generally slower, making them suitable for home or public charging stations where vehicles can be plugged in for extended periods. Both types cater to different charging needs and infrastructure scenarios.</p></p>
