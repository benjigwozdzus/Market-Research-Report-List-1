<p><ul><li>Non-Scanning LiDAR</li><li>Scanning LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical sensors for LiDAR market is primarily divided into two types: Non-Scanning LiDAR and Scanning LiDAR. Non-Scanning LiDAR employs a fixed array of sensors to capture data in a broad swath, making it ideal for applications needing a wide coverage, such as topographical mapping. In contrast, Scanning LiDAR uses oscillating laser beams to systematically scan the environment, allowing for high-resolution 3D mapping and detailed object recognition, which is crucial for industries like autonomous vehicles and forestry.</p></p>
