<p><strong>The Cellphone Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IPS</li><li>ASV</li><li>TFT</li><li>OLED</li><li>STN</li><li>CSTN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellphone display market encompasses various technologies, each with unique characteristics. IPS (In-Plane Switching) offers superior color accuracy and viewing angles, while ASV (Advanced Super View) enhances this with better contrast. TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) provides improved response times but has limited viewing angles. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) delivers vibrant colors and deeper blacks, allowing for thinner designs. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) and CSTN (Color Super Twisted Nematic) are older technologies, offering lower quality and slower refresh rates, mainly used in budget devices.</p></p>
