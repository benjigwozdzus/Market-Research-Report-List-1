<p><p>The medical angioplasty balloons market comprises various types designed for specific therapeutic purposes. Normal balloons are used for standard dilation of narrowed arteries. Drug-eluting balloons are coated with medication to prevent restenosis post-procedure. Cutting balloons feature microscopic blades to incise the arterial wall for better expansion. Scoring balloons have predetermined grooves that facilitate controlled vessel dilation. Each type addresses unique clinical needs, enhancing treatment efficacy for cardiovascular and peripheral vascular conditions.</p></p>
