<p><p>The advanced power wheelchair market comprises three main types based on wheel configurations: rear-wheel drive, front-wheel drive, and mid-wheel drive. Rear-wheel drive provides excellent speed and efficiency on flat surfaces, making it ideal for outdoor use. Front-wheel drive offers superior maneuverability and is well-suited for navigating tight spaces indoors. Mid-wheel drive combines the benefits of both, allowing for a tighter turning radius and stability, making it versatile for various environments, including both indoor and outdoor scenarios.</p></p>
