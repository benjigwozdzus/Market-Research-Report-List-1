<p><p>The Smart Thermal Control System market comprises two primary types: Passive and Active Smart Thermal Control Systems. Passive systems utilize materials and design features to naturally regulate temperature without external energy input, improving efficiency through thermal insulation and heat management. In contrast, Active systems incorporate technology like sensors and automated control mechanisms to actively manage temperature by adjusting heating or cooling elements in real-time. Both types aim to enhance energy efficiency, comfort, and performance in various applications, from buildings to electronic devices.</p></p>
