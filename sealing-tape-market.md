<p><p>The sealing tape market is segmented into single-layered and multi-layered types. Single-layered sealing tape consists of one layer of adhesive material, offering basic sealing capabilities suitable for general applications. In contrast, multi-layered sealing tape incorporates multiple layers, enhancing durability, strength, and resistance to environmental factors. This type is often preferred for demanding applications, such as in construction or packaging, where a robust seal is essential. Both types serve distinct needs across various industries, ensuring efficient sealing solutions.</p></p>
