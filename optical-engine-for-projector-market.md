LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) uses liquid crystal panels for improved resolution and color depth. LED projectors utilize light-emitting diodes for compact size and energy efficiency. Other types may include hybrid or emerging technologies, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences in the projection market.</p></p>
