<p><strong>The Photonic Infrared Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photonic infrared sensors can be categorized into three types based on their wavelength of operation. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) sensors operate in the 1,000 to 2,500 nm range and are used for imaging and sensing applications in various industries. Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR) sensors, with wavelengths from 3,000 to 5,000 nm, are commonly utilized in thermal imaging and surveillance. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) sensors, operating from 8,000 to 14,000 nm, are primarily employed in temperature measurement and nighttime vision applications.</p></p>
