<p><p>The lubricated vacuum pumps market is segmented into two main types: single stage and multi stage. Single stage pumps are designed for applications requiring lower vacuum levels and are often simpler in construction, making them cost-effective and easier to maintain. In contrast, multi stage pumps utilize multiple sets of pumping mechanisms to achieve higher vacuum levels, making them suitable for more demanding industrial processes. Both types are vital in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing.</p></p>
