<p><ul><li>Active Systems</li><li>Passive Systems</li><li>Hybrid Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature Controlled Packaging Systems ensure the safe transit of temperature-sensitive products. Active systems utilize energy sources like batteries or refrigerants to maintain specific temperatures, providing precise control during shipping. Passive systems rely on insulating materials without active elements, preserving temperatures through thermal inertia and advanced design. Hybrid systems combine both technologies, offering flexibility and efficiency by adapting to varying conditions. This market addresses critical needs in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food sectors to ensure product integrity throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
