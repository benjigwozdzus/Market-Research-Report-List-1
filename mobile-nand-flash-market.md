<p><ul><li>Vertically Stacking</li><li>Photolithography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile NAND Flash market consists of two main types: vertically stacking and photolithography. Vertically stacking, or 3D NAND, involves layering memory cells to enhance storage density and performance without increasing the footprint, allowing for more efficient mobile devices. Photolithography, on the other hand, uses light to etch patterns on silicon wafers, enabling the production of smaller, faster chips. Both methods target the demand for higher capacity and speed in smartphones and tablets, driving advancements in mobile technology.</p></p>
