<p><p>The medical air conditioner market comprises three main types: rooftop, classic, and inverter systems. Rooftop air conditioners are installed on rooftops and provide efficient ventilation for large medical facilities. Classic models are traditional split systems, ideal for smaller clinics and hospitals, offering reliable cooling and heating. Inverter air conditioners, known for their energy efficiency, adjust compressor speed to maintain steady temperatures, making them suitable for environments requiring precise climate control, such as operating rooms and intensive care units.</p></p>
